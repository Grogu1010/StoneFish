Stonefish v6.5 final test results

Build contents:
- Stonefish v6.5
- Stonefish v6.5 Flash
- Existing v1-v6 engines retained
- Shared version-aware openings.js retained and expanded with v6.5-only lines
- test_engines65.js
- benchmark_speed65.js

Design constraints checked:
- No nominal ply was added or removed from the v6.5 versions.
- v6.5 Flash keeps the v6 Flash/v6-level tactical profile but uses a faster candidate-narrowed cached pass.
- v6.5 full keeps the full v6 ply profile and uses staged finalist verification, cache-heavy scoring, opponent-plan suppression, and conversion pressure.
- Mate-in-one and avoiding opponent mate-in-one remain absolute checks.
- Openings remain in the shared openings.js file; v6.5-only lines use minStonefishVersion: 6.5, so older engines ignore them.

Additional v6.5 features:
- v6.5 fast tactical cache and reusable board signatures.
- More aggressive candidate narrowing without reducing ply.
- Plan-prevention scoring: candidates are rewarded for reducing the opponent's next forcing plan.
- Book-safe fast path for trusted v6.5 opening continuations.
- Stronger conversion pressure and anti-repetition handling while ahead.
- Draw rescue behavior retained when worse.
